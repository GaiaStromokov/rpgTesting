#Classes 
Classes are profession trees

Each being a collection of both active and passive abilities one can purchase with class exp. Effectively turning each class into a collection of feats

Up to what would be considered 5th level is open to any, thought after thst the conditions for buying more points increase, insentiving creativity while not making them mandatory for gameplay

All characters will also have the ability to invest their exp into the generalized skill tree as well, 
Effectively everybody being a fighter
